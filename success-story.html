Enabling International Expansion for Boë Gin to land in Europe. (T7)

Not all brands are able to land in Europe and scale up rapidly… with the help of Global Listings, Boë Gin was able to do just that.

OUR DELIVERABLES

  • Market Research
  • Demand Forecasting
  • Global Listings enrolment
  • International Expansion Expertise
  • Launch & Scale strategies

Having already found ample success in the UK, launching within Europe was a clear next step for Boë Gin in order to further dominate the platform. Unicorn launched Boë Gin in the German market first after market research confirmed a high level of demand.

Market Research Leads to Successful Expansion.

After determining that Germany demonstrated the highest volumes, we assisted our client with applying for a VAT and EORI number through our partner.

Amazon Global Listings Program.

Once VAT was assigned within DE, we contacted Amazon in order to have our client enrolled within the Amazon Global Listings program, ensuring all criteria were met.
